About me

Currently based between Barcelona and Berlin, I offer my services to help people create and shape their music. For my personal projects, I've produced under several names such as Alain Gertrand, Garfive, Cuff Link, Andrew & His Assistant, Elias Tutto…

I’m devoted to making soulful music that can convey the artist’s intentions. I really pride myself on the psychological and social aspects of my job: to me, the relationship I build with my clients and collaborators is essential to the quality of what we make. Making music together is also about talking, taking time to understand each other, building trust, feeling free to experiment and to express ideas…

If you feel identified with this way of working, you’re in the right place!
